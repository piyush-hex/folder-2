Chapter 1 – Computer System

A computer system is an integrated assembly of hardware and software components designed to process data and perform a variety of tasks. At its core, a computer system consists of the central processing unit (CPU), memory (RAM), storage devices (like hard drives and SSDs), and input/output devices (such as keyboards, mice, and monitors). The hardware works in conjunction with system software, primarily the operating system, which manages the computer's resources and provides a user interface. Computer systems can vary widely in complexity, from simple embedded systems in household appliances to powerful supercomputers used for scientific research.
